jGooooo - Travel Platform

jGooooo

jGooooo is a comprehensive travel search engine with global ambitions, like Google for travel . Still, the search information is systematized and carefully selected for the purpose of saving and simplifying the search. Searching for information and planning trips for users, integrated with a specialized social network for sharing and connecting about journeys, as well as travel support features such as booking, and booking tours.
